Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the website lacible.de, which is operated by Lacible Advisory, a company registered in Germany.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data, and what rights you have in relation to your personal data.

What personal data do we collect?

We do not collect any personal data from you when you visit our website. We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies to monitor your online activity or behavior.

We only collect personal data from you when you contact us directly, for example, by email, phone, or through our contact form. The personal data we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide us.

How do we use your personal data?

We use your personal data only for the purpose of responding to your inquiries, providing you with our services, or fulfilling our contractual obligations to you.

We do not use your personal data for any other purposes, such as marketing, advertising, or profiling, without your prior consent.

How do we share your personal data?

We do not share your personal data with any third parties, unless we are required to do so by law, or with your express consent.

We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our website, such as hosting, maintenance, or security services. These service providers may have access to your personal data, but only to the extent necessary to perform their tasks, and under strict confidentiality and security obligations.

How do we protect your personal data?

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encryption, firewalls, and other security technologies to safeguard your personal data.

We store your personal data on secure servers located in Germany, and we comply with the GDPR and other applicable laws regarding data protection and cross-border data transfers.

How long do we keep your personal data?

We keep your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, or as required by law. We delete your personal data when it is no longer needed, or upon your request.

What are your rights regarding your personal data?

You have the following rights regarding your personal data, subject to certain limitations and exceptions:

  • Right of access: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data that we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data that we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to request that we delete personal data that we hold about you.
  • Right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer your personal data to another organization, or directly to you.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of our processing based on your consent before its withdrawal.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority of your country or region.
